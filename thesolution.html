The solution

We want to build recycling centres in the outskirts of all cities across the whole world. Not only the big cities, but also in between smaller cities to make the solution accessible for everyone.

These centres will be easy to use, and we will develop a database with a user-friendly software which tracks the amount of trash you individually have recycled.

For those with smartphones we will also develop an app which will do the same thing, but you will have access to see your amount without going there physically, as well as a map to see where your closest centre is located.

In these centres you can submit your waste, either plastic or glass by recycling. In these centres you can submit your waste, either plastic or glass by recycling. When you have recycled a given amount of trash you get a given amount of score/currency. This score or currency can be used in our centres to retrieve a reward. The reward depends on your wish, it can be anything from bread to real life money.

In short, gathering plastic and glass will give the citizen a reward. This solution will be worth it for everyone, since it affects you as an individual and the world for the better.

NB! To assure us that this process does not result in anyone exploiting other people. Our centres will have a max weight recycled per month at 25 kg.

Benefits

  • A solution to minimize the amount of plastic pollution.
  • More plastic will be re-used, reduces waste generation.
  • Raises the awareness of pollution in general.
  • Supports developing countries by increasing their capacity for recycling.
  • A new workplace for interested citizens.
  • Gives the average citizen an economical boost.
  • Might affect the poverty and hunger rate positively because of giving everyone equal opportunities.

Challenges

  • It will have a major effect on the local market.
  • Can be problematic building recycling centers in areas with bad infrastructure.
  • Financially expensive.
  • Maintaining enough employees.

Connecting the solution and problem to a Sustainable Development Goal

Our solution will contribute in various amounts towards most of the sustainable development goals. The ones we will have the largest impact on is:

  • 11. Sustainable cities and communities
  • 12. Responsible consumption and production
  • 13. Climate action
  • 14. Life below water

The development goal our solution will contribute the most towards is number 12: Responsible consumption and production.
We will be a company that handles huge amounts of plastic- and glass waste. And we will contribute towards a better circular economy
by developing better recycling habits for people as well as production of recycled goods.
